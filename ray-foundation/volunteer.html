Become a Volunteer

Join our mission to help kids thrive! Volunteering at The Ray Foundation is a rewarding experience where you can make a real difference in the lives of children.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a positive impact in your community.
  • Gain valuable experience in child development and non-profit work.
  • Meet like-minded individuals and build meaningful connections.

How to Get Started?

  1. Fill out our volunteer application form.
  2. Attend a brief orientation session.
  3. Choose your area of interest, from tutoring to event planning!
