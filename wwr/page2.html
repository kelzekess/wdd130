Our Story

The Ray Foundation is dedicated to supporting children in need by providing resources that help them grow and succeed. Our mission is to create a future where every child can thrive. With an emphasis on education, emotional well-being, and community involvement, we strive to empower children to reach their full potential.

Our Core Programs

We focus on three main areas that are crucial for the development of children: Education, Sports, and Emotional Support.

Education Programs

Our education programs include after-school tutoring, mentorship, and literacy workshops. We aim to bridge the education gap by providing children with the tools to excel academically.

Sports Activities

Our sports programs help children develop teamwork, leadership, and discipline. From soccer leagues to basketball teams, children can engage in physical activities that promote a healthy lifestyle.

Emotional Support

Our emotional support services offer counseling, therapy, and group support for children dealing with trauma, anxiety, or other challenges. We strive to create a safe space where children can express themselves freely.
