Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this a good trip for kids? What are the age ranges you allow?

Absolutely! This trip is suitable for children of all ages. However, children over the age of 8 tend to enjoy the experience more. The river offers stunning views and exciting rapids that keep everyone entertained, and guides are always there to ensure safety and fun!

Is whitewater rafting safe? I don't even know how to swim! Can I still come?

Whitewater rafting does carry some inherent risks, but safety is our top priority. All guides are Wilderness First Aid certified, and each person wears a Coast Guard-approved life vest. Many non-swimmers have a fantastic time rafting, and guides will provide an engaging safety talk before the trip begins. The most common injuries are minor things like sunburns!

What is included in the trip?

We provide all the necessary camping equipment, including sleeping bags, tents, chairs, utensils, and a waterproof duffle bag. Water and lemonade are included during the day, and you'll enjoy coffee, tea, and hot chocolate in the morning and evening. We also provide 3 meals per day, plus snacks. You can bring your own drinks (beer, liquor, soft drinks), within reasonable limits.

What do I need to bring?

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For the best availability, especially during peak seasons, we recommend booking your trip 6 to 12 months in advance. If you're flexible with your dates, 6 months in advance is often sufficient.
