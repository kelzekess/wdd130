Our Educational Programs

At The Ray Foundation, we are committed to offering a wide range of programs that help children and young adults develop valuable skills for their future. Through education, teamwork, and leadership, we aim to empower the next generation.

After-School Tutoring

Our after-school tutoring program focuses on providing children with the academic support they need to succeed. Whether it's math, science, reading, or writing, our trained tutors work closely with students to strengthen their understanding and boost their confidence. We aim to bridge learning gaps and ensure that no child is left behind.

Goals: Improve academic performance, foster a love of learning, and provide a supportive environment for children who need extra help with schoolwork.

Technology Training Workshops

Our technology training workshops are designed to give children the tools and skills they need to thrive in an increasingly digital world. From coding and app development to digital literacy and problem-solving, we offer a variety of courses that teach practical skills. These workshops are designed to be interactive and engaging, helping children build a foundation in tech that will benefit them in the future.

Goals: Teach coding, foster innovation, and provide children with the digital literacy needed for success in school and future careers.
